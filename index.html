About Me

Top

I am a Computer Science student studying at Portland Community College in Portland Oregon, earning a transfer degree in order to finish my Bachelors in CS at Portland State University. My educational and professional goals are to become a UI developer for both native apps and the web. My passion for technology reaches back to my early teens when I made maps (levels) for the popular shooter series ‘Quake’.

After high-school life took me in a fully creative direction as a touring musician. During the last 8 years I have been actively touring in bands, and working for independent record labels as an inventory coordinator. The wild ride through the world I experienced as a musician helped me forge strong goal oriented and team based collaboration skills which I can apply directly to software development.

My current goal is to get freelance web development work while attending college, where the final goal is for me to become a versatile and knowledgeable full stack developer. Ultimately, I am currently available and seeking work. I am always looking to meet new people, be they developers, potential clients or just to geek out on technology. Feel free to contact me through the various networks linked in the ‘Networking & Sharing’ section of this site, or directly via e-mail. – Nicholas Fazzolari
